and #[Isa. 24:11] wail, all you drinkers of wine,

# ch. 2:2 powerful and beyond number;
# [Rev. 9:7, 8] its teeth are lions’ teeth,
and it has the fangs of a lioness.

and splintered my #[ver. 12]fig tree;

# See Hos. 4:3 the ground mourns,
because #[Hos. 2:9]the grain is destroyed,
# [Hos. 2:9] the wine dries up,
the oil languishes.

# ver. 17 because the harvest of the field has perished.
12The vine dries up;
# [ver. 7] the fig tree languishes.
Pomegranate, palm, and apple,
all the trees of the field are dried up,
and #Isa. 24:11; Jer. 48:33gladness dries up
