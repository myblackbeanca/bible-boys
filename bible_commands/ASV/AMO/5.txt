LABEL:3
VERSE:For thus saith the Lord Jehovah: The city that went forth a thousand shall have a hundred left, and that which went forth a hundred shall have ten left, to the house of Israel.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For thus saith Jehovah unto the house of Israel, Seek ye me, and ye shall live;
LABEL:5
VERSE:but seek not Beth-el, nor enter into Gilgal, and pass not to Beer-sheba: for Gilgal shall surely go into captivity, and Beth-el shall come to nought.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Seek Jehovah, and ye shall live; lest he break out like fire in the house of Joseph, and it devour, and there be none to quench it in Beth-el.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Ye who turn justice to wormwood, and cast down righteousness to the earth,
LABEL:8
VERSE:seek him that maketh the Pleiades and Orion, and turneth the shadow of death into the morning, and maketh the day dark with night; that calleth for the waters of the sea, and poureth them out upon the face of the earth (Jehovah is his name);
LABEL:9
VERSE:that bringeth sudden destruction upon the strong, so that destruction cometh upon the fortress.
LABEL:10
VERSE:They hate him that reproveth in the gate, and they abhor him that speaketh uprightly.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Forasmuch therefore as ye trample upon the poor, and take exactions from him of wheat: ye have built houses of hewn stone, but ye shall not dwell in them; ye have planted pleasant vineyards, but ye shall not drink the wine thereof.
LABEL:12

LABEL:14
VERSE:Seek good, and not evil, that ye may live; and so Jehovah, the God of hosts, will be with you, as ye say.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Hate the evil, and love the good, and establish justice in the gate: it may be that Jehovah, the God of hosts, will be gracious unto the remnant of Joseph.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Therefore thus saith Jehovah, the God of hosts, the Lord: Wailing shall be in all the broad ways; and they shall say in all the streets, Alas! alas! and they shall call the husbandman to mourning, and such as are skilful in lamentation to wailing.
LABEL:17
VERSE:And in all vineyards shall be wailing; for I will pass through the midst of thee, saith Jehovah.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Woe unto you that desire the day of Jehovah! Wherefore would ye have the day of Jehovah? It is darkness, and not light.
LABEL:19
VERSE:As if a man did flee from a lion, and a bear met him; or went into the house and leaned his hand on the wall, and a serpent bit him.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Shall not the day of Jehovah be darkness, and not light? even very dark, and no brightness in it?
LABEL:21

LABEL:22
VERSE:Yea, though ye offer me your burnt-offerings and meal-offerings, I will not accept them; neither will I regard the peace-offerings of your fat beasts.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Take thou away from me the noise of thy songs; for I will not hear the melody of thy viols.
LABEL:24
VERSE:But let justice roll down as waters, and righteousness as a mighty stream.
LABEL:25
VERSE:Did ye bring unto me sacrifices and offerings in the wilderness forty years, O house of Israel?
LABEL:26
VERSE:Yea, ye have borne the tabernacle of your king and the shrine of your images, the star of your god, which ye made to yourselves.
LABEL:27
VERSE:Therefore will I cause you to go into captivity beyond Damascus, saith Jehovah, whose name is the God of hosts.
